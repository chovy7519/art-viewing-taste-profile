# Xiaohongshu Sharing Assets

Goal: win attention with a concrete before/after and a shareable personality card.

## Required Assets

1. Cover image: personality card plus 2-3 exhibition photos.
2. Before/after image: messy album grid versus generated artwork cards.
3. Result card: 4-letter aesthetic personality, Chinese name, one-line verdict, keywords.
4. 30-second recording: upload photos, recognition, aesthetic judgment, personality card.

## Title Patterns

- "看完展后，我用 AI 把一堆照片整理成了审美人格"
- "我做了一个 AI 观展 Skill：不用拍展签，也能生成作品卡"
- "这些展览照片，暴露了我的审美人格"

## 30-Second Recording Outline

0-5s: show the problem: exhibition album is full of artwork photos, details, and duplicates.

5-12s: drop photos into the Skill; explain that labels are optional and the model tries to identify works from image evidence.

12-20s: show artwork candidates, confidence, and one concise aesthetic judgment.

20-27s: show the aesthetic personality card.

27-30s: call for seed users: "如果你也看展拍很多照片，我想找 20 个种子用户一起测。"

## Copy Guardrails

- Do not oversell as perfect recognition.
- Emphasize evidence, correction, and personal taste discovery.
- Say "审美人格不是给你打分，而是描述你会被什么样的美吸引。"
